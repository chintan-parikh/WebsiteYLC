About Us




Young Leaders’ Consortium or YLC as the name suggests is a gathering of young student leaders from diverse educational fields who can share their skills, experience, knowledge among themselves and spread it to various student communities in India, as thus provide exposure of leadership to all parts of the country.





  • Aim
  • Vision
  • Mission

YLC aims to offer a lively and memorable learning opportunity for students seeking a supplement to their education and develop the leadership skills within them by nurturing the traits required for attaining excellence through a wide variety of well planned activities in a challenging environment and by giving them exposure of various leadership programs.

In a country like India, talent is plenty but opportunities are very less and YLC wants to provide this opportunity to the students in India. The goal of YLC is to work extensively towards the aspiration of modern youth, especially through the continued expansion and enhancement of the organization through national and international collaborations.

YLC focuses for the promotion of student interest in international relations and related subjects, the increase in capacity for students to engage in problem solving, the teaching of various conflict resolution methods, research skills, and communication skills, and the creation of opportunities to meet and interact with people from diverse fields and build long lasting relationships among the youth community.

Odisha

With the above vision in mind, Young Leaders’ Consortium began its operations in its first chapter Odisha couple of months after the inception of Young Leaders' Consortium India to provide access to our programmes to student communities across Odisha. Along with that, we have engaged in collaborations in several other cities, where we would be expanding soon. In Odisha, we developed a team of exuberant and dedicated people. Their tremendous efforts in consolidating several student communities in order to provide exposure to leadership led to the establishment of YLC Odisha. The team has been working together with several institutions and organizations operating in Odisha and the rest of eastern India, expanding Young Leaders’ Consortium’s operational base, allowing youngsters from a variety of backgrounds to identify their leadership potential.

Chennai

Chennai, which earlier was core base for the Young Leaders’ Consortium has now been converted into a core chapter, with a team already put in place. Chennai core chapter has already conducted MUN workshops and Leaderships workshops in SRM University and SSN College of Engineering, NIT Trichy etc. The Chennai core chapter also extends to projects in nearby places of Vellore, Trichy and Coimbatore. The Chennai chapter of YLC aims to spread the idea of leadership among schools of Chennai to that the students have a solid base for becoming leaders of tomorrow.

Mumbai

The Mumbai core chapter of Young Leaders’ Consortium has been formed seeing the tremendous potential that is present in this part of the country. The Mumbai chapter shall act as the base for all projects in Nagpur, Pune and Ahmedabad as well. Several workshops and MUN associations are already lined up under the Mumbai chapter, and many more in pipelines. The enthusiasm with which the team of Mumbai chapter has started their work has been commendable and promises to bring out the best leaders from the region.

Bangalore

The Bangalore chapter of Young Leaders’ Consortium is one among the three new chapters that have been set up under Young Leaders’ Consortium India, and aims to tap the potential of the brightest minds and the ever expressive youth of the city of Bangalore. The team for the Bangalore chapter is in a developing stage, however at this initial stage they have shown a lot of promise and spreading the message of Leadership loud and clear in the area of work.

Hyderabad

The Hyderabad chapter is the newest chapter under Young Leaders’ Consortium India. Hyderabad has shown lot of scope for nurturing and growth of leaders and thus we have entered with YLC into Hyderabad. The Hyderabad core chapter will work as base for other areas as well, which include Warangal, Vijaywada. MUN workshop and MUN association projects are already in place and with many other projects to come, YLC aims to nurture the best of leaders from Hyderabad.
